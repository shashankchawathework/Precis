About the Creator

Meet Shashank Chawathe, the mind behind Précis

Shashank Chawathe

Shashank Chawathe is a final-year BBA LL.B student at IFIM Law School, Bengaluru, passionate about making legal knowledge accessible and understandable for students and professionals alike. With a keen interest in legal research, writing, and public speaking, Shashank aims to bridge the gap between dense legal judgments and practical learning through Précis.

Inspired by the complexities of legal texts and the challenges faced by students and professionals in navigating case law, he created Précis as a platform to summarize landmark judgments in a concise, structured, and reader-friendly way. Every summary is designed to provide clarity, insight, and an easy understanding of legal principles.

Mission

The mission of Précis is to empower law students, professionals, and curious minds with clear and reliable legal knowledge. By transforming complex judgments into digestible summaries, Shashank envisions a platform where learning from landmark cases becomes seamless, engaging, and impactful.
