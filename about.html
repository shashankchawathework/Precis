About Précis

Précis is an initiative by Shashank Chawathe, a final-year BBA LL.B. student at IFIM Law School, Bengaluru. It simplifies complex legal judgments into concise summaries for students and professionals.

Each summary covers Facts, Issues, Judgment, and Legal Principles for clarity and quick reference.
