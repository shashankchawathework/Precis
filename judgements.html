Judgement Summaries

Kesavananda Bharati v. State of Kerala (1973)

Established the Basic Structure Doctrine, limiting Parliament’s power to amend the Constitution.

Maneka Gandhi v. Union of India (1978)

Expanded the interpretation of Article 21 — Right to Life and Personal Liberty.

Vishaka v. State of Rajasthan (1997)

Laid down guidelines for protection of women from sexual harassment at the workplace.

Navtej Singh Johar v. Union of India (2018)

Decriminalized consensual homosexuality by reading down Section 377 IPC.
